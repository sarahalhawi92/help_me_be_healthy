What do I need to know about fibres?

Fibre is not a nutrient and contains no calories or vitamins.Fibre helps your digestive system to process food and absorb nutrients.
Fibre can help to lower blood cholesterol. Fibre makes you feel fuller and so helps to control your appetite.
